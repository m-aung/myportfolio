Uprising ORM

Dev Tool for Deno

dORM is an uber-lightweight postgreSQL query builder for Deno and is currently being expanded into a full-fledged object-relational mapping (ORM) tool. Its purpose is to make your life easier when making SQL queries and let you write queries in familiar Javascript/Typescript syntax and dot notation.
